## Core Platform Features

### Customer-Facing Capabilities

#### Shopping Experience
- **Product Catalog**: Advanced browsing with filters, search, and categorization
- **Product Details**: Rich descriptions, specifications, reviews, and image galleries
- **Shopping Cart**: Persistent cart with guest checkout support
- **Wishlist Management**: Save products for future purchases
- **Quick View**: Fast product preview without page navigation

#### Customer Accounts
- **User Registration**: Simple sign-up with social login options
- **Order History**: Complete purchase tracking and reorder capabilities
- **Address Management**: Multiple shipping and billing addresses
- **Profile Management**: Personal information and preferences

#### Content & Engagement
- **Blog Platform**: Content marketing and customer education
- **Product Reviews**: Customer feedback and rating system
- **Contact Forms**: Direct customer communication channels
- **Newsletter Subscription**: Marketing communication opt-ins

### Administrative Capabilities

#### Product Management
- **Complete Product Control**: Add, edit, and manage inventory
- **Category & Brand Organization**: Structured product taxonomy
- **Image Management**: Multiple product images with optimization
- **Pricing Flexibility**: Dynamic pricing, discounts, and promotions
- **Specification Management**: Detailed product attributes

#### Order Processing
- **Order Management**: Complete order lifecycle tracking
- **Payment Processing**: Multiple payment method support
- **Shipping Management**: Integration with shipping providers
- **Order Status Updates**: Real-time customer notifications
- **Returns & Refunds**: Customer service workflow support

#### Marketing & Promotion
- **Coupon System**: Discount codes and promotional campaigns
- **Banner Management**: Homepage and promotional banners
- **Blog Management**: Content marketing platform
- **Email Marketing**: Customer communication tools

#### Business Intelligence
- **Sales Analytics**: Revenue and performance metrics
- **Customer Insights**: Purchase behavior and preferences
- **Inventory Reports**: Stock levels and movement tracking
- **Order Analytics**: Processing times and fulfillment metrics
